About Pilipinas Meta Inc.

Pilipinas Meta is building Pilipinas-verse, the envisioned virtual world of the Philippines where citizens interact in a mixed reality setting, creating real world and virtual world impact. We aim to bring Filipinos to the Web 3.0 piece-by-piece, virtual landmark by virtual landmark.

Pilipinas Meta offers the creation of an immersive, real-world environment, where players can interact and transact in an-all digital space and economy, and where challenges, quests, and rewards are provided.
